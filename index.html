An inspection tool built around you.

You know every unit. You catch what's wrong. You make the call. What you shouldn't have to do is type the same notes twice.

This platform reads inspection notes the way you naturally write them — on your phone, in plain language — and converts them into structured work orders ready for your company's paper trail. No template to fight with. No fields to memorize. Your voice, their format.

your notes → auto-extracted → you confirm → ready to print

Your notes become work orders. The retyping goes away.

What this is built to solve
  • The hour-after-inspection tail where notes turn into paperwork
  • The attention cost of re-keying unit numbers, issues, and priorities
  • The mismatch between how you naturally write and what the company form demands
  • The gap between the time you're given and the workload you're handed
How it works
  1. Write inspection notes the way you already do.
  2. Hand them to the platform — it extracts units, issues, categories, priority.
  3. Review each order. Nothing commits until you confirm.
  4. Generate the PDF batch. Print, email, or archive.
This system is yours to shape. How you use it, what you ask of it, what you change — all of it informs what gets built next. You are not just a user of this tool. You are the first one.

Runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is saved, sent, or uploaded.

What this is designed around
Your notes stay your notes. No template to fight with, no required fields. The tool adapts to your writing, not the other way around.
You see the parse before the paper. Nothing prints until you've signed off on each order. The tool reads and suggests — you decide.
Output matches your current paper trail. Same form, same format, same routing. Only the re-keying goes away.
Non-work-order items get routed. Complaints and lease notes don't become work orders — they're flagged separately, so nothing falls through.
